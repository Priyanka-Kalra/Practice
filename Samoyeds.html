Description

The Samoyed (/ˈsæməjɛd/ SAM-ə-yed or /səˈmɔɪ.ɛd/ sə-MOY-ed;Russian: Самое́дская соба́ка or Самое́д) is a breed of medium-sized herding dogs with thick, white, double-layer coats. They are a spitz-type dog which takes its name from the Samoyedic peoples[*] of Siberia. Descending from the Nenets Herding Laika, they are a domesticated animal that assists in herding, hunting, protection and sled-pulling. Samoyed dogs are most often white, and can have a brown tint to their double-layer co which is naturally dirt-repellent. They are known to be used in expeditions in both Arctic and Antarct regions and have a friendly and agreeable disposition.

Characteristics:

  • Cute
  • Fluffy
  • Grumpy
  • Lazy
  • They drink H2O
  • They become extremely dirty when outside
